# Statistical Methods Reference

Use design-appropriate diagnostics rather than a one-size-fits-all checklist.

## DID And Event Study

- pre-treatment pattern
- staggered-timing estimator choice
- treatment-timing support

## IV

- first-stage strength
- instrument relevance
- exclusion-restriction discussion
- interpretation of the parameter being estimated

## RDD

- bandwidth choice
- manipulation checks
- local-balance diagnostics

## Matching Or Reweighting

- overlap
- balance before and after weighting
- sensitivity to trimming
