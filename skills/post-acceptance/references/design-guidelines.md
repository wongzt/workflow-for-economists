# Design Guidelines for Seminar And Policy Materials

## Slide Design

- use a clean widescreen ratio
- keep margins and typography consistent
- use readable type at room scale
- prefer high-contrast colors
- keep charts simple and easy to read in grayscale

## Poster Or Handout Rule

If the user needs a poster or handout, emphasize:

- clear hierarchy
- minimal text blocks
- large labels
- one central result per visual region

## Policy Brief Rule

- lead with the question and main result
- explain the identifying variation in plain language
- avoid technical notation unless necessary
- keep claims conditional when external validity is limited
