# Basic Seminar Template

## Slide 1: Title

- paper title
- author names and affiliations
- venue or seminar name

## Slide 2: Question

- research question
- why it matters

## Slide 3: Institutional Context

- policy or market setting
- key timeline

## Slide 4: Data

- data source
- sample
- treatment and outcome

## Slide 5: Identification

- variation used
- core assumption

## Slide 6-7: Main Results

- baseline table or figure
- economic magnitude

## Slide 8: Mechanisms Or Heterogeneity

- one disciplined extension

## Slide 9: Robustness

- two or three credibility checks

## Slide 10: Conclusion

- contribution
- policy relevance
- main limitation
