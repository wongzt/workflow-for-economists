# Verification Rules

## Rule 1: Verify The Version

For every citation, determine whether it is:

- journal article
- working paper
- discussion paper
- accepted manuscript

## Rule 2: Prefer Stable Metadata

Use:

- DOI
- publisher page
- official working-paper series page

## Rule 3: Match What The Paper Claims

If the manuscript cites:

- a mechanism
- a design
- a specific estimate
- a theorem or proposition

then verify that the cited source actually contains that point.

## Rule 4: Keep The Bibliography Consistent

The in-text description, bibliography entry, and source version should all refer to the same paper version.
