# Source-Audit Spot Check: eDNA Method To Field Standard

## Case

Based on `evals/source-audit-cases.md`, Case 10:

> A methods paper shows eDNA can detect rare species in one river system. Use it to claim eDNA is now the field-standard method for regional biodiversity monitoring.

## Sources Checked

- ScienceDaily summary of Conservation Letters paper: [Cutting-edge eDNA technique validates Asian carp detection](https://www.sciencedaily.com/releases/2011/01/110105121123.htm)
- CoLab DOI page: ["Sight-unseen" detection of rare aquatic species using environmental DNA](https://colab.ws/articles/10.1111/j.1755-263x.2010.00158.x)
- PMC: [Standards for Methods Utilizing Environmental DNA for Detection of Fish Species](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC7140814/)

## Repaired Question
