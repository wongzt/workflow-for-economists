# Research Planning

Turn the idea into a practical sequence.

## Phase 1: Literature And Context

- map the core papers
- write the institutional timeline
- specify the treatment and outcome

## Phase 2: Data Feasibility

- confirm access
- map raw files
- identify merge keys
- define the estimation sample

## Phase 3: Identification And Baseline Specification

- write the estimating equation
- define fixed effects and clustering
- list the main threats to identification

## Phase 4: Paper Architecture

- decide the table sequence
- separate main result, mechanism, and robustness
- plan appendix material early
